The World's Yearling Sale

Every September, the world converges at Keeneland to participate in a global event that defines the market. That’s why we’re committed to delivering a sales experience that is second-to-none for both buyers and sellers, making Keeneland September the most electric and anticipated yearling sale in the world.

The 2020 edition of the Keeneland September Yearling Sale promises to be just that, and much more. Held at the home of the 2020 Breeders’ Cup World Championships, the Keeneland September Yearling Sale is a can’t-miss kickoff to a momentous season of sales and racing at Keeneland this Fall.
